Welcome to SDGs Open Data Portal

Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) have been the defined as the global development goals by UN till 2030. So, underlining this fact, the organization has felt the necessity of building outreach of SDGs through the use of technology via the concept of Open source data on SDGs project in Nepal. Open data is the idea that some data should be freely available to everyone to use andrepublish as they wish, without restrictions from copyright, patents or other mechanisms of control. In this data portal, there will be the list of districts in Nepal where SDGs projects are running, their time frame, funding size, donor agencies, the objective of the project, and progress till now, the number of beneficiaries of the project. Through this data portal, people will be aware of the progress of the SDGs project going in their respective areas, know about the funding size and agencies (which helps in Accountability) and citizen will monitor online and if not progress reach on time,the data portal will even update recommendation from the expert. This project also engages CSO and citizens. This data portal can be accessed through the website.
